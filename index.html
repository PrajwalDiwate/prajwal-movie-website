Yash

Naveen Kumar Gowda, known professionally as Yash, is an Indian actor and one of Kannada cinema’s biggest stars, acclaimed nationwide for his role as Rocky Bhai in the K.G.F film series.Yash began his career with appearances in several television series in the 2000s.
